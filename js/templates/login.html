Hello, welcome to Leon's Hotel Management System. Ver 1.0

This system handles room and guest management, reservations, room charges, payments, room availability, and user management.

You can generate the following reports :

  • List of Reservations
  • List of Check-Ins
  • Folio Record
  • List of Payments Received from Guests

Use the form on the left to log in and start using the system.
